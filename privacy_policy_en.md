# CubeSolver Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

CubeSolver is a local 3x3 Rubik's Cube scanning, solving, and teaching tool. We minimize data collection. Core recognition, calibration, solving, and animation playback are performed on the user's device.

## Information We Collect and Use

### Camera

CubeSolver requires camera permission to recognize cube stickers in real time. Camera frames are used only for local recognition. Photos, videos, and camera frames are not uploaded.

### Local Cube Profiles

CubeSolver stores user-confirmed color recognition profiles on the device to improve recognition stability for the same cube in future scans. Users can export or import cube profiles from the settings page.

### Last Scan Record

CubeSolver stores the most recent solvable 6-face scan on the device so users can restore progress after restarting the app. This record is not uploaded.

### Voice and Sound Settings

CubeSolver stores local settings such as the sound switch. Voice prompts use the phone system's text-to-speech capability.

## Information We Do Not Collect

The current version does not collect accounts, contacts, location, photo library data, advertising identifiers, payment information, or analytics data.

## Data Sharing

The current version does not upload or share user data with third-party servers. Cube profile files exported by the user are managed and shared by the user.

## Data Deletion

Users can delete local data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data. Exported backup files are managed by the user.

## Children's Privacy

CubeSolver does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The core app features do not require account login.
